Gesture Activity Monitor (live — PAJ7620U2 raw detections)

Per-direction hit counts from the current journal. Use this to see which physical swipes the sensor actually detects: if a direction stays at 0 while you swipe it, the PAJ7620U2 is not registering that motion (a sensitivity / field-of-view problem, not a mapping bug).

Click Refresh to load

For reliable detection (per DFRobot PAJ7620U2): keep your hand 5–15 cm from the sensor (≤20 cm max), swipe deliberately and fully across the sensor's field of view (a complete pass, not a flick), and pause briefly between gestures. A direction that consistently fails usually means the hand is leaving the sensor's cone before the swipe completes — re-aim or unblock that side of the sensor.

Sensor LED Indicators (liveness check)

Turn on a Person Sensor's on-board indicator LED. Lighting it is an I2C write to the sensor, so a lit LED proves that sensor is powered and reachable on its bus — the most direct alive/dead test for these irreplaceable sensors. Both LEDs ship disabled in firmware; toggling here is live and persisted to SD.

Requires the S150d firmware on each Teensy (REPO-ONLY until flashed). After flashing, toggle on: if the LED does not light, that sensor is not reachable on its I2C bus (power/wiring), not merely flaky.
